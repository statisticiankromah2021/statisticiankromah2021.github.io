About Me

I earned a Bachelor's of Science (BSc) degree in Mathematics from the University of Liberia. I also earned a Bachelor of Science (BSc) and a Master (MSc) degrees in Statistics from the prestigious Ecole Nationale Superieure de Statistiques et d'Economie Appliques in Abidjan, Cote d'Ivoire. Since 2021, I have been working at the Liberia Institute of Statistics and Geo-Information Services (LISGIS) as Principal Analyst and Technical Advisor in the Office of the Deputy Director General for Statistics and Data Processing.
